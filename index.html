Dine at Fetch

Welcome to Fetch. A premium coffee house serving quality food made from the highest of ingredients. Located in the heart of New York surrounded by insipring and young people. We aim to provide the best experince for you and your dog.

Before you dine...
We love dogs at Fetch, here are some tips to make sure you have the best dining experience for you, and your furry friend.
Keep your dog on a leash.
Don't let your dog run around! Keep them on a leash and close to you and your table. You will be asked to leave if your dog is wandering around the restaurant without you.
Make sure your dog is trained.
Don't bring your new dog that's not trained! If your dog is aggressive towards people and other dogs you will be asked to leave.
Make sure your dog is fed.
A hungry dog, is a grumpy dog. Make sure your dog is on their best behavior while dining. Don't bring your yapping aggressive dog inside.
